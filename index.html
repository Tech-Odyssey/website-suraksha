Your Safety
Now, In Your Hands

In the recent years, Many instances of crimes against Women,children and old people have been coming to our notice. Each day the severity of these crimes is reaching new levels of insanity. We had to find a solution for the public, hence we decided to make our own take on the solution.

Suraksha, solves this problem by having a full-fledged SMS Alert system, informing your family and firends in times of need. We also utilise ML to predict the danger zone areas, helping you pre-plan your travels and avoid danger.
